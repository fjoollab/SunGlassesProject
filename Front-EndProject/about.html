Who are we?

CARA is a global eyewear brand committed to providing the latest fashion and eye-wear products in all possible price ranges. We offer a vast portfolio of premium products, and our main target is casual sunglasses for every day usage, but we also sell safety glasses, prescription lenses as well as fashion goggles. CARA was founded in 2019 with the goal of making quality eyewear available to everyone at any time. Sunglasses are not just worn to protect your eyes; they should be fashionable and different. Design is driven by customer needs and wants. We work closely with our customers by listening and satisfying their demands while simultaneously exploring new trends together.

Putting on a new pair of glasses or sunglasses is a simple way to completely transform your look - just like a new hairstyle.
